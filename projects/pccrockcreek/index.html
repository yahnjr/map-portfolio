Drone Orthophotography Workflow at PCC Rock Creek Campus

Skills used:

While obtaining a Part 107 license for operating drones in a commercial setting, I had the opportunity to utilize the DJI Phantom to fly a small portion of one of the PCC campuses. This storymap showcases the full workflow for obtaining high definition, georeferenced orthophotography using an sUAS: from mission planning, to execution, to post processing. The software used to process and measure the final products was Agisoft.
