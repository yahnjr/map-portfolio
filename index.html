    About Me

    My name is Ian Maher. I am currently working as a GIS Technician with 3J Consulting, a Portland, Oregon-based engineering and urban planning firm. I love making maps and analyzing spatial data. I believe all aspects of bringing spatial data to people, including cartography, application development, and communication, are important facets of what makes GIS special.

    I love exploring the world at every scale. Some of my hobbies include scuba diving, coding, making music, hiking, reading, and traveling. I am passionate about animals and the environment, and I hope my work can one day bring about a better world.
